How To Delete Holidays From Samsung Calendar 2024

Introduction

With the advancement of technology, we have all become heavily reliant on our digital calendars to keep track of our schedules. However, with the inclusion of holidays, our calendars can become cluttered and difficult to navigate. In this article, we will discuss how to delete holidays from your Samsung Calendar in 2024.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Open Samsung Calendar

The first step to deleting holidays from your Samsung Calendar is to open the app on your device. You can do this by locating the app icon on your home screen or by searching for it in your app drawer.

Step 2: Go to Settings

Once you have opened the app, click on the three horizontal lines in the top left corner. This will open the menu. From there, click on the gear icon to access the settings.

Step 3: Select Calendar

After clicking on settings, select the option for “Calendar” from the list of options. This will bring up a list of all the calendars that are currently active on your device.

Step 4: Uncheck Holiday Calendar

Locate the option for “Holidays” in the list of calendars. Once you find it, click on the box next to it to uncheck it. This will remove all of the holidays from your calendar.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will this delete all of my events?

A: No, this will only remove the holidays from your calendar. Your other events will remain intact.

Q: Can I still add events for holidays?

A: Yes, you can still add events for holidays manually if you would like.

Q: Will this delete holidays from future years?

A: No, this will only remove holidays from the year 2024. If you want to remove holidays from future years, you will need to repeat this process for each year.

Conclusion

By following these simple steps, you can easily remove holidays from your Samsung Calendar in 2024. This will eliminate clutter and make it easier for you to navigate your schedule.
